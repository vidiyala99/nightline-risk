# NIGHTLINE RISK: NIGHTLIFE GENERAL LIABILITY POLICY v2026.1

## SECTION I: COVERAGES

### 1.1 BI bodily injury and property damage liability
We will pay those sums that the insured becomes legally obligated to pay as damages because of "bodily injury" or "property damage" to which this insurance applies. We will have the right and duty to defend the insured against any "suit" seeking those damages.

### 1.2 LIQUOR LIABILITY (DRAM SHOP)
This insurance applies to "bodily injury" or "property damage" arising out of the selling, serving or furnishing of any alcoholic beverage.
**Condition:** The insured must maintain a valid liquor license and comply with all local ordinances regarding service hours.

## SECTION II: EXCLUSIONS

### 2.1 ASSAULT AND BATTERY EXCLUSION
This insurance does not apply to "bodily injury" or "property damage" arising out of:
(a) The actual or threatened assault or battery by any person;
(b) The failure of any insured or anyone else for whom any insured is legally responsible to prevent or suppress such conduct.
**EXCEPTION:** This exclusion does not apply if the insured has opted for the "Security Performance Rider" (See Section IV).

### 2.2 EXPECTED OR INTENDED INJURY
"Bodily injury" or "property damage" expected or intended from the standpoint of the insured is excluded.

## SECTION III: MANDATORY SECURITY CONTROLS

### 3.1 ID SCANNING REQUIREMENTS
The insured is required to use an electronic ID scanning system for all patrons entering after 9:00 PM. Failure to maintain scan logs for a minimum of 90 days will result in a 25% increase in the deductible for any related claim.

### 3.2 STAFFING MINIMUMS
A minimum ratio of 1 security guard per 100 patrons is required during peak hours (10:00 PM - Close).

## SECTION IV: SECURITY PERFORMANCE RIDER

### 4.1 DE-ESCALATION COVERAGE
If the insured can provide video evidence of attempted de-escalation by licensed security personnel, the "Assault and Battery Exclusion" is waived for that specific incident.
